Sara's Top Movies of All Time

My top three movies are:

After

"After" (2019) is a romantic drama directed by Jenny Gage, based on Anna Todd's novel. It follows college student Tessa Young as she falls for the mysterious Hardin Scott, uncovering secrets and facing emotional turmoil. The film explores love, betrayal, and self-discovery, appealing to a young adult audience.

Black Honey "Aasal Aswad"

"Black Honey" (Asal Eswed) is a 2010 Egyptian comedy-drama directed by Khaled Marei. It stars Ahmed Helmy as Masry, who returns to Egypt after 20 years in the U.S., facing humorous and poignant challenges while reconnecting with his roots and navigating cultural differences.
